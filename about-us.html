About us

Bad & Boogie was started in 2017 by Alvin Zedendahl, initially as a comment and reaction to the all-style battles that were pervasive in Sweden at the time. The event is a concept battle as such, although it may seem that the concept is too simple, it is the simplicity of the concept that lends it strength. Battles limited to a singular style challenged by a plethora of music of different genres ranging from jazz to baile funk to future bass and more! It has so far been a success in all categories, venues, and cities that event has encompassed. This year is the biggest ever - even taking place in the Royal Castle in Stockholm.

Our Goals

In the age of Information where everything moves at frightening speed on a daily basis and new TikTok dances flood the web in a manner of seconds it can easily seem as if older cultural expressions will be forgotten. We see this more as a challenge and a new opportunity, by challenging dancers new and experienced to compete within the scope of their chosen style to wide variety of music (including mainstream songs) we believe that it connects the dance to current movements. This can help the styles and cultures stay relevant and healthy for long!
