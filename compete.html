Hip Hop and Popping Reinforced

Our concept is simple but strong, we have the classic battle format strengthened by a musical challenge. The dancers will battle it out in a set style to unpredictable genres and songs, helping them reach new levels of musicality and creativity. Our DJs always avoid the typical battle songs and spend hours digging for music to surprise the dancers and audience with.

A celebration of culture past and current

This years categories: Hip Hop 2vs2 and Popping 1vs1

In this edition of Bad & Boogie we will have brought not only great music and dance to the table- we have secured great prizes for the winners! The winner of the Popping category gets 10.000kr in prize money, and the winners of the Hip Hop category gets 10.000kr each as well!

Surprise prizes!

As soon as you enter the dance floor you have the chance to win a cash prize! Each year we have "hidden" categories ranging from the best dressed, the best cyphering and more. This year is no different, so show up ready for anything!
