This years competition

Categories:

This year we are going strong with Hip Hop 2vs2 and Popping 1vs1. Click here to find out more.

Schedule:

The event consists of two days, the 11th June and the 12th June. On the first day you will see the preliminaries and the very first battles of the event as well as some special appearances.

The 12th June is the day of the finals. You will see amazing battles, hear amazing music, and have an amazing time. This evening will also have showcases, judge solos and a live music performance.

You are welcome here!

Bad & Boogie are open for people who love dance and music, a rich and diverse community is the backbone of great dance, and a great time. We follow Swedish regulations for dB-levels so young children can come and join us, as well as making sure that the event is alcohol and drug free. For the adults who want to party we have an afterparty after the finals!

Going to compete?

Whether you make it to the final day or not we have a reduced price for all competitors who want to experience the whole event. It also includes tickets to the workshops and the afterparty!
